Incognito mode Turn on Incognito mode in Chrome, Search, YouTube and Maps First launched in Chrome, Incognito mode has since become available in our most popular apps. In YouTube, Search on iOS and Maps, just tap from your profile picture to easily turn it on or off. When you turn on Incognito mode in Maps and YouTube, your activity, like the places that you search for or the videos that you watch, won't be saved to your Google Account. Browsing history and cookies from your Incognito session are deleted from Chrome once you close all Incognito windows.
