Some of our datasets YouTube-8M YouTube-8M A large-scale labeled video dataset that consists of 8 million YouTube video IDs, deep inception-3 features, and associated labels from a diverse vocabulary of 4800 visual entities. Deep Model Generalization (DEMOGEN) Deep Model Generalization (DEMOGEN) The DEMOGEN dataset consists of 756 trained deep models, along with their training and test performance on the CIFAR-10 and CIFAR-100 datasets Open Images Open Images A dataset consisting of ~9 million URLs to images that have been annotated with labels spanning over 6000 categories. Google Landmarks Google Landmarks Contains images annotated with labels representing human-made and natural landmarks. The dataset can be used for landmark recognition and retrieval experiments. AudioSet AudioSet Comprehensive sound ontology with over 600 sound classes and a dataset of over 2 million 10-second YouTube clips human-annotated with sound labels. Natural Questions Natural Questions A question answering dataset. Text of real anonymized, aggregated queries issued to the Google search engine. Robotic Tasks Robotic Tasks A collection of examples of a robot arm pushing and grasping a variety of objects. The dataset contains RGB-D views of the arm, gripper and objects, along with actuation and position parameters. Nsynth Nsynth A large-scale and high-quality dataset of annotated musical notes.
